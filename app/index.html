Diversify Hacking was built to ignite change and promote equality in the tech industry.   Women make up less than 25% of the tech industry. Imagine how much we could grow if we applied the same diversification logic as other industries. 

This is revolutionary folks.

ABOUT

We are ladies in the tech industry passionate about encouraging more diversity. Research proves that an industry rich with diversity grows, innovates and pushes the boundaries of current status quo. With greater diversity, the opportunities in this industry are endless. 

Don’t miss our first annual summer hackathon, dedicated to bringing the women mobile developers in Atlanta together to create mobile apps in purpose of helping Georgia’s sex trafficking victims and their families. Atlanta is rated #1 in the country for sex trafficking. We are proud to join in the huge group effort to bring about awareness and stop the craziness.

Human trafficking statistics:

  • Over 5,000 girls are at-risk of being sex trafficked in Georgia every year.
  • According to a former FBI agent, Atlanta has an internal trafficking problem, where minors, many recruited from the city, are transferred to another county or state.
  • Approximately 374 girls are commercially sexually exploited monthly in Georgia. The average age of entry into the commercial sex market for girls is a staggering 12 and 14 years.
  • Each month, 12,400 men in Georgia pay for sex with an underaged girl and 7,200 of them end up exploit an adolescent females.
